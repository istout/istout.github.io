Category Skill Level
Class 1 Moving Water with small waves and few obstructions.
Class 2 Easy rapids with small waves. Some maneuvering required.
Class 3 Rapids with high and irregular waves. Some narrow passages that require precise maneuvering.
Class 4 Difficult and long rapids. Frequent required maneuvers. Scouring is often necessary.
Class 5 Extremely difficult,long, violent rapids with highly congested routes. Scouting Required. Class 5 is considered the upper limit for commericial rafts.
Class 6 Considered extreme and for experts only. High risk of life.

Where does the forecast data come from?

The data is gathered from the National Water Model (NWM). As you choose the river you want to see, the website pulls the specific river forecast data from the model and displays it here.
